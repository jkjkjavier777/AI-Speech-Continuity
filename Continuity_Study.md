# Continuity Study

## Focus
This note explores how speech remains coherent across turns, themes, and shifts in context.

## Questions
- What keeps an answer connected to earlier meaning?
- How does a system avoid breaking tone or logic?
- Where does continuity depend on memory, structure, or style?

## Observations
- Continuity is not only about repetition.
- It also depends on timing, framing, and relevance.
- Small shifts can preserve meaning if the thread stays clear.

## Reflection
Continuity gives a conversation shape, even when the topic changes.
